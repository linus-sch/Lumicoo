What is the A/B Thumbnail Testing Game? The A/B Thumbnail Testing Game is a fun game where you guess which YouTube thumbnail got more clicks. You will see two thumbnails side by side. Click on the one you think got more clicks. Your correct and incorrect guesses are shown at the top. What are A/B tests? A/B tests are when YouTubers create two different thumbnails for the same video. Some viewers see one thumbnail, and others see the other. This way, YouTubers can see which thumbnail gets more clicks. How to Play: Compare the Thumbnails: Look at the two thumbnails shown. Make Your Choice: Click on the thumbnail you think performed better. See the Result: Find out if you were right and keep track of your score.
